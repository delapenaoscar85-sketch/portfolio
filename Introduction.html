INTRODUCTION

I, Cesar Iyan C. Ligutan, a student teacher of Datu Lipus Makapandong National High School, aim to showcase my internship journey in this portfolio. Since I began to step into the life of being an educator, I learned that teaching is not just a source of knowledge but also a fountain of values and professionalism. It has been a long journey—challenging at times, yet truly worth it. During these two months, I was given the opportunity to experience what it really means to be a teacher. I learned that teaching is not only about delivering lessons, but also about understanding students, managing different behaviors, and creating a positive learning environment.

At the beginning of my internship, I felt nervous and uncertain of myself. I was worried about how I would face the students and whether I would be able to teach effectively. However, as the days went by, I slowly became more comfortable. Through observation, practice, and guidance from my cooperating teacher, I started to gain confidence in handling the class.

I experienced both successes and challenges during my internship. There were moments when my lessons went well and students actively participated, which made me feel proud and motivated. At the same time, there were also difficulties, such as managing noisy students or explaining lessons clearly. These experiences taught me to be patient, flexible, and open to improvement.

This portfolio presents my journey as a student-teacher. It includes my weekly journals, professional readings, and teaching documents that reflect my growth and learning. Each experience helped me develop not only my teaching skills but also my character as a future educator.

To sum up, my internship helped me realize that teaching is a meaningful and noble profession. Despite the challenges, it is a career that requires passion, dedication, and continuous learning. I am thankful and elated for this experience as it prepared me to become a more competent and responsible future educator equipped with both personal and professional growth, discipline, and integrity in the field of education.
